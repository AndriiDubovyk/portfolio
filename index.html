About Me

Hello, my name is Andrii Dubovyk and I am a student with a passion for Android development. I have been studying and practicing Android development for the past year, and during this time, I have created several projects for myself to hone my skills.

I am particularly interested in mobile app development, and I am constantly striving to learn more about the latest technologies and techniques in this field. I have a strong foundation in Java and Kotlin, and I am proficient in using Android Studio to develop and deploy applications.

In addition to my technical skills, I am also a creative problem-solver and a team player. I enjoy collaborating with others to create innovative solutions to complex problems. I am excited about the potential of Android development to make a positive impact on people's lives, and I am eager to continue building my skills in this field.

Projects

  • Word Snap

    Flashcards for learning English words. There are various auxiliary functions, such as notifications, backup of cards through Firebase, automatic definition of words from the dictionary API, pronunciation of words through text-to-speech technology.

  • Bookend

    Very minimalistic and fast e-book reader app. Created with Java and XML. A lightweight library MuPDF was used for book pages rendering.
